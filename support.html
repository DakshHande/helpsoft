📩 HelpSoft Support – Get Assistance!

We offer support via **email only** to ensure organized and professional responses. Please read the guidelines below before reaching out.

⏳ Approximate Reply Time

  • 📌 General Queries – **24 to 48 hours**
  • 📌 Service-Related Questions – **12 to 24 hours**
  • 📌 Urgent Matters – **6 to 12 hours** (Subject to priority)

📅 When to Mail Us?

  • ✅ **Monday to Saturday** – **9:00 AM to 6:00 PM IST**
  • ❌ **Sundays & Public Holidays** – No support available
  • ✅ Mail us **only for genuine guidance, service-related concerns, or paid service clarifications.**

🚫 When NOT to Mail Us?

  • ❌ Outside working hours or holidays.
  • ❌ Spam, promotional emails, or unnecessary queries.
  • ❌ Personal requests unrelated to our services.
  • ❌ Asking for backend access, user login, or sign-up help (We do not have these features).

📜 What to Mail?

  • ✔ Service inquiries (Free & Paid Services).
  • ✔ Website guidance-related questions.
  • ✔ Issues regarding meeting fees (₹50 for agents, ₹150 for CEO).
  • ✔ Reporting errors, broken links, or bugs.
  • ✔ Business inquiries & partnerships.

❌ What NOT to Mail?

  • 🚫 Login/Signup issues (We do not have user accounts).
  • 🚫 Personal relationship advice, legal disputes, or confidential matters.
  • 🚫 Technical help unrelated to our platform.
  • 🚫 Any illegal, harmful, or unethical queries.

📧 How to Mail Us?

Follow the proper email format for quick resolution:

Subject: [Issue Type] – Short Description

Body:

  • ✅ Your Full Name
  • ✅ Contact Email
  • ✅ Clear & Concise Message
  • ✅ Screenshots (if needed)

📩 **Email:** support@helpsoft.com

🔒 Privacy & Confidentiality

All emails are **strictly confidential**. We do not share, sell, or misuse user inquiries.

For detailed privacy policies, visit Privacy Policy.

📢 Final Note

We aim to provide **top-quality guidance** while maintaining a structured support system. Kindly be patient while we respond to your queries.
